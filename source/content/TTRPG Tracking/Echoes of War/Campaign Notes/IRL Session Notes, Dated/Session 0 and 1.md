Took place 2-21-26, in-game Ches 21st 1487

Began with the Party (sans [[Forge Unbroken]] for now) being joined together by [[Grandsire Na’thaniél]]'s fierce determination to get [[Ascánthir Crithatri]] to get involved with an adventuring party.

[[Ember Blackthorn-Fenwick]] and her husband [[Rook Fenwick]] had been in [[Hilp]] for awhile, and sought to collect some mail from noble relatives.

[[Vaelithra Igristisk]]'s motives for being in [[Hilp]] remained obscure, but an affiliation with Bahamut was established by a solid Knowledge Roll by [[Ascánthir Crithatri]].

[[Ysgraviir Ysraldsson]] was similarly vague about his goals in [[Hilp]], but was quick to share his lack of respect for [[Cormyr]] when involved in a conversation with [[Ven Kallix]].

The Party was united through a fight that broke out outside the Stag Skull Inn in [[Hilp]]. The combat was ended when [[Ven Kallix]] succeeded in casting Sleep on an enemy, allowing the Party to interrogate him. This creature, named Grub, revealed his crew's purpose was to kill the inhabitants of [[Hilp]] by poisoning the local well by the wishes of their master, a young Green Dragon named Friroriror, or "The Green Death".
