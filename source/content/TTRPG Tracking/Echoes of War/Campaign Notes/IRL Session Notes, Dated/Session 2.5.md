"Sands and Shadows"
[[Ascánthir]] solo quest, took place in the realm of Ches 22nd.

According to [[Ascánthir]] in a conversation in [[Gladehaven Keep]] in [[Session 4]]:

He went to a Suzailian University where he used to be an adjunct 25 years ago, desiring to clean up a bit. Being ambushed by the Red Wizards of Thay, he was killed, teleported to Thay(?), and resurrected for the purposes on interrogation.

The Wizards questioned [[Ascánthir]] about the Book of Vile Darkness, a tome said to contain knowledge of how to become a Lich. [[Ascánthir]] is a pro under pressure and doesn't crack.

Back in [[Suzail]], [[Brannon]], having avoided capture by the Red Wizards' ambush, seeks out assistance from [[Mistress Ossani Eveningspire]] in rescuing [[Ascánthir]]. After doing so, she takes him to Eveningstar, where they lent aid to [[Tinuvian]], who was in the midst of a Red Wizard scrape-up as well. In the end, some explosions happened, the Emerald Enclave took care of business, and the group teleported to [[Gladehaven Keep]]'s stable.
